Product Description - According to hook model
But why Hook Model?

The aim is to influence customers to use your product on their own, again and again, without relying on overt calls-to-action such as ads or promotions and create unprompted user engagement.

As I am making product for millenials which are very much impatient, have low attention span, so a reliable platform with rich and optimum content can be very helpful for them.

Also, thus can be helpful from a business point of view as users can be hooked to our product and which can generate us revenue even more.

For our product, following hook model blindly was not a good option as the platform is not entirely a habit forming product. Using bits and pieces of the hook model to enhance experience is what I did in this project.


1. Trigger:
A trigger is the actuator of behavior. Once user has the motivation to refer about his/her investment decision our landing page works as the first trigger.

It shows about all motivations of the users which are fulfilled by the product. A four fold landing page scratches the user motivation and gains trust so as to make them take an appropriate action.

Apart from this once user has started using the product they are given personalized and custom notifications to again trigger the action. These notifications could be anything related to quests, alerts, etc. Also these notification helps in effective consumption of the information.

2. Action:
Action is an essential element of the habit forming product but it needs sufficient motivation and ability to perform an action. So for sufficient motivation we have listed things on our landing page. i.e. Earning more money, effective and efficient way to understand market, and thus less risk factor.

To increase the ability of the user to take action, platform is simplified and made more understandable. Onboarding and go through of the product after sign in/sign up makes it more obvious for the users to use the platform.








Now, Functionalities which user can use as a part of his/her actions are:
i) Reading News


ii) Observing and knowing Stock listings
  • Real time data.
  • Analysis curated by our experts.
  • Top picked relevant news by our analysts.
  • Predefined mathematical models to keep a track by yourself


iii) Learning about market and finance




iv) Knowing latest alerts



v) Using Investment simulation


vi) And, registering for premium services


Cognitive shortcuts are used in the product to increase the likelihood of their desired action. So as to give them better understanding of market and finally help them in their investment decisions.

Framing effect: Use of appropriate graphics (and not just plain text) with color-coding to represent things in a very fine way. This way making it more interesting and increasing chances of content surfing and consumption.

Endowed progress effect: Giving 10 investwiser coins just after login and mentioning that user will need a minimum of 20 coins to start investing in the simulator. This will motivate user to answer quest in the learn section to earn investwiser coins.
3. Variable Reward:
Rewards of the hunt:

Feed is the section which every user experience once entered on the platform. The stream of limitless information displayed in a scrolling interface makes for a compelling reward of the hunt. It‚Äôs filled with a mix of both mundane and relevant content.

This variety creates an enticingly unpredictable user experience. On occasion, a user might find a particularly interesting piece of news, while other times, he/she won‚Äôt. But to keep hunting for more information, all that is needed is a flick of the finger or scroll of a mouse.

Feed contains:
  • Easy to read content from all over the product.
  • Quick links to main features.

Basically providing with all the required information at one place in a short format so that user can be engaged and can surface content more smoothly. This was introduced in the product because the attention span of the targeted user is very low.


Rewards of the Self:

For more personal form of gratification quizzes and investwiser money are there in the product. Also earning through simulator will also give instant gratification.

Maintaining variability in rewards: Quizzes can be of different types, the money rewarded by that can vary and earning money from simulator is unpredictably variable as it is based on real market stats.

  • Learning from module which requires investing time.
  • Simulator section which requires investment of money as well as time.
  • And, If pro account taken then investment of real money which is a major binding factor to the product.
